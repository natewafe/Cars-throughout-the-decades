Gallery IV, 2000s

Bugatti
Veyron

One thousand horsepower. Four wheel drive. Street legal.

Gallery IV, 2000s Volkswagen Group handed Bugatti a specification that physics had to catch up to.
Eight liters, sixteen cylinders Quad-turbo. Ten radiators. A drivetrain designed to survive itself.
Michelin and Brembo Tires rated for fifteen minutes at full speed. Then replace.
Autocar, 2005 “A car that, in motion, felt almost calm despite its statistics.”
